Radio Transmitter

How does a radio actually work? Through this project, I gained a deeper understanding of the subject. The goal was to build an AM radio transmitter using two main components: an audio transformer and a 1 MHz crystal oscillator.

Photos

The pictures above show the 2 components soldered and connected via wires on a perforated board. The first picture on the left shows the two components: on the left, the audio transformer, and on the right, the crystal oscillator. The second picture shows the soldered joints on the back of the perforated board.
